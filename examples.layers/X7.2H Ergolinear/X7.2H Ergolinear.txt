X7.2H Ergolinear kbd layout by Ian Douglas
(same alphab as X7.1H, reworked puncs (?))

http://www.keyboard-design.com/
see also http://shenafu.com/smf/index.php?topic=89.0

One big 'special feature' of this layer is the fact that H is on alt-space (on thumb).
I have to admit that it really does not work well on my US kbd adaptation.
Hopefully it will make much more sense when I try it on a matrix kbd.


-- Main --

OS   q y o u _              b m r c z Ins
Tab  ' i e a j              w t n s k #
Cap  : p < g >              v d l f x Alt
Ctl          CR Sp    AltGr Sh        Ctl

(OS = Win)

(PQ, For Matrix, add Ctrl to right of Sp and to left of AltGr !)
 2x3 thumb keys :  CR Sp Ctl     Ctl AltGr Sh


-- Shift --

Mnu  Q Y O U @              B M R C Z 
     ~ I E A J              W T N S K 
     | P ^ G \              V D L F X
                H                          

(Mnu = menu)


-- AltGr / punc --

Esc  [ $ ? = ]              & { } + ` 
     " ( . , !              * ; - ) / %
     8 4 0 2 6              9 3 1 5 7
                h
